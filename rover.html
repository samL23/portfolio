Project
Overview

As Mechanical Co-lead, I was responsible for the end-to-end design of the rover's chassis, suspension, and drivetrain systems. The work spanned concept development, ANSYS FEA validation, GD&T engineering drawings, composite fabrication, and competition testing. Below are the individual sub-projects that made up the mechanical scope of this build.

SUB-PROJECTS

Mechanical work,
broken down.

CHASSIS IMAGE
01 · STRUCTURAL DESIGN

Chassis Design & FEA

Designed the main rover chassis from scratch in SolidWorks, optimizing for stiffness-to-weight ratio under competition loading conditions. Ran ANSYS static structural analysis to validate weld joints and frame members under worst-case terrain impacts. Iterated the design through three major revisions based on simulation results and fabrication feedback.

SolidWorks ANSYS FEA Structural Analysis Welded Frames
SUSPENSION IMAGE
02 · MOBILITY SYSTEM

Rocker-Bogie Suspension

Designed and validated a rocker-bogie passive suspension system enabling the rover to traverse obstacles up to twice wheel height without active control. Optimized link geometry for ground clearance and stability on inclined terrain. Produced full GD&T engineering drawings for machined components and sourced hardware for fabrication.

Mechanism Design GD&T SolidWorks Terrain Analysis
WHEEL IMAGE
03 · NOVEL COMPONENT

Non-Pneumatic Wheel

Led development of a compliant non-pneumatic wheel to eliminate the puncture risk inherent to inflated tires in rocky terrain. Explored lattice spoke geometries in SolidWorks and validated compliance and load-bearing capacity through FEA. Prototyped iterations using FDM 3D printing and composite layup before arriving at the final design.

Compliant Mechanisms ANSYS FEA Composites FDM Prototyping
DRIVETRAIN IMAGE
04 · POWER TRANSMISSION

Drivetrain & Motor Mounts

Designed the drivetrain integration connecting six brushless DC motors to the wheel hubs, including custom motor mounts, gearbox interfaces, and cable management routing. Validated mount stiffness under peak motor torque loading with FEA. Coordinated with the electrical sub-team to ensure thermal clearance and connector accessibility.

Drivetrain Motor Integration ANSYS FEA DFM
TESTING IMAGE
05 · VALIDATION

Test Planning & Validation

Authored the mechanical validation test plan covering static load tests, terrain traversal trials, and endurance runs. Defined pass/fail criteria tied to FEA predictions and competition requirements. Led field testing sessions, logged failure modes, and fed findings back into design iterations before final competition submission.

Test Planning Validation Technical Documentation Team Leadership
